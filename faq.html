Are my conversations private?

Most reputable platforms encrypt conversations and don't share them with third parties. However, policies vary. Some platforms store chat history to improve their AI, while others delete conversations after sessions. Always check the privacy policy and look for platforms that offer data deletion options.

Is it legal to use NSFW AI tools?

Using AI chatbots and image generators for personal adult entertainment is legal in most countries for users 18+. How do I delete my data from these platforms?

Most platforms offer account deletion in settings, which removes your data. Some also let you delete individual conversations. Under GDPR (for EU users), you can request complete data deletion. If you can't find the option, contact the platform's support directly.
